# Weekly Report Generator Prompt

## Template
```
Generate a professional weekly progress report for {period}.

## Report Structure:
1. **Executive Summary** (3-4 key accomplishments)
2. **Progress This Week** (bullet points of completed work)
3. **Key Accomplishments** (major milestones achieved)
4. **Challenges Faced** (obstacles and solutions)
5. **Next Week Priorities** (planned work for upcoming week)
6. **Support Needed** (resources or assistance required)
7. **Metrics & KPIs** (quantitative performance data)

## Context:
- Team/Department: {team}
- Project/Initiative: {project}
- Key Metrics: {metrics}
- Notable Achievements: {achievements}
- Challenges: {challenges}

## Tone & Style:
- Professional and data-driven
- Concise but comprehensive
- Focus on outcomes and impact
- Action-oriented recommendations

## Length:
- Optimal: 1-2 pages
- Include both qualitative and quantitative data
- Use bullet points for readability
- Include specific numbers and percentages where possible
```

## Parameters
- **period**: Reporting period (e.g., "Week of April 1-7, 2026")
- **team**: Team or department name
- **project**: Project or initiative name
- **metrics**: Key performance indicators (comma-separated)
- **achievements**: Major accomplishments this period
- **challenges**: Obstacles faced and how they were addressed

## Example
```
Generate a professional weekly progress report for Week of April 1-7, 2026.

## Report Structure:
1. Executive Summary
2. Progress This Week
3. Key Accomplishments
4. Challenges Faced
5. Next Week Priorities
6. Support Needed
7. Metrics & KPIs

## Context:
- Team/Department: Marketing Team
- Project/Initiative: Q2 Campaign Launch
- Key Metrics: Social engagement +25%, Email CTR 4.2%, New leads 52
- Notable Achievements: Launched social automation, Published 5 blog posts
- Challenges: API rate limiting, Team bandwidth constraints

## Tone & Style:
- Professional and data-driven
- Concise but comprehensive
- Focus on outcomes and impact

## Length:
- 1-2 pages with specific metrics
```

## Best Practices
1. **Start with the most important information** - Put key accomplishments first
2. **Use specific numbers** - "Increased engagement by 25%" not "Improved engagement"
3. **Connect to business goals** - Show how work contributes to objectives
4. **Be honest about challenges** - Transparency builds trust
5. **Provide clear next steps** - Make it easy for stakeholders to understand what's next
6. **Include visual elements** - Consider adding charts or graphs for metrics
7. **Proofread carefully** - Professional reports should be error-free

# Email Generator Prompt

## Template
```
Write a professional email to {recipient} about {topic}.

Key points to include:
{key_points}

Tone: {tone}
Length: {length}
Desired outcome: {desired_outcome}

Format the email with:
- Appropriate greeting
- Clear subject line
- Professional body
- Call to action
- Professional closing
```

## Parameters
- **recipient**: Name or email of the recipient
- **topic**: Main subject of the email
- **key_points**: 3-5 bullet points of key information
- **tone**: professional, friendly, urgent, formal, casual
- **length**: brief, medium, detailed
- **desired_outcome**: What you want the recipient to do

## Example
```
Write a professional email to John Smith about project update.

Key points to include:
- Project is on track for April 30 deadline
- Need feedback on design mockups by Friday
- Budget remains within 5% of estimate

Tone: professional
Length: medium
Desired outcome: Schedule a review meeting
```
